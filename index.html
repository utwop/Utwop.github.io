About Me

Ricardo De Jesus is a current student at the College of Westchester, studying Interactive Design and Marketing. He currently specializes in graphic design, video editing, and social media marketing, and has higher aspirations of becoming a college professor. He wishes to teach about the social impacts of digitalization, it's marketing implications, and potential consequences.
